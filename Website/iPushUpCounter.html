iPush-Up Counter

iPush-Up Counter is a perfect way to count the number of push-ups you do in a workout session. All you have to do is place your device on the ground, and on every push-up repetition tap the button with your nose!

App Features

  • Counts the number of push ups in single session.

  • Records the total amount of push-ups the whole time you have this app!

  • Choice of sound settings. With more sounds to come!

  • Set your own rep goals.

  • Keep track of how many goals you have completed.

  • Toggle vibrate option.

  • Track your rep goals completed.
