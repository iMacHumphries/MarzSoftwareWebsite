iPush-Up Counter Pro

If you like iPush-up Counter, you will love iPush-Up Counter Pro! iPush-Up Counter Pro is the perfect way to count the number of push-ups you do in a workout session. All you have to do is place your device on the ground, and on every repetition the Proximity Sensor will count your push-ups keeping a life time total!

App Features

  • Uses iPhone Proximity Sensor to count push-ups!

  • Custom themes!

  • No ads!

  • Counts number of push-ups in a single session.

  • Records the total amount of push-ups for life!

  • Choice of sound settings. With more sounds to come!

  • keep track of how many goals you have completed.

  • toggle vibrate option.

  • track how many rep goals you have completed.
