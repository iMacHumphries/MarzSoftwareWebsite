NYRC Devotionals

NYRC Devotionals are a series of devotionals that are written by serveral different people. They are perfect for anyone, whether you know Jesus Christ as your personal savior, or you are just learning about Him for the first time. NYRC stands for New Year Renewal Campaign. Its original purpose was to strengthen the Covington Bible Church, but now we would like to share it with the world.

Goals:

  • To become confident in our standing with the Lord.

  • To be able to share the simple, but clear Gospel message with another person. 1 Peter 3:15 says that each of us should be able to "give a reason for the hope . . . "

  • To understand what Christ has done for us, and respond in obedience to the Scripture's entreaties to present ourselves to Him.

App Features:

  • 26 Great Devotionals.

  • Easy note taking system.

  • All indicated passages are available in the New American Standard Bible for you if you do not have internet access.

  • BibleGateway.com provides many different versions of the bible passage to chose from within the app.

  • More updates to come!
