iSit-Up

iSit-Up is a perfect way to count the number of sit-ups you do in a workout session and keep a running total of lifetime sit-ups. All you have to do is place your device on the ground behind your head, and on the completion of each sit-up bring your head close to the proximity sensor on your camera! A sound will assure you that your sit-up was counted.

App Features:

  • Counts the number of sit-ups in single session.

  • Records the total amount of sit-ups the whole time you have this app!

  • Choice of sound settings, with more sounds to come!

  • Set your own rep goals.

  • Keep track of how many goals you have completed.

  • Toggle vibrate option.
